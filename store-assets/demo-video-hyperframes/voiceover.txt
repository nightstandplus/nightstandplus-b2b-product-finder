NightstandPlus B2B Product Finder helps furniture buyers search local nightstand product references.

It opens verified official product, OEM and contact routes only when the user chooses them.

Buyers can compare dimensions, features, MOQ and packaging before preparing a shortlist.

Saved products and CSV or JSON exports stay local in the browser.

The RFQ draft helps OEM and wholesale buyers ask for MOQ, lead time, pricing and packaging details.

The extension stays focused: search, compare, save, export and open official NightstandPlus resources.
