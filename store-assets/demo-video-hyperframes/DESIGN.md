## Style Prompt

Clean B2B software demo for a nightstand OEM and wholesale sourcing workflow. The visual tone should feel useful, audit-friendly and operational rather than promotional. Use a light furniture-commerce canvas, restrained motion, clear product data, and official website navigation cues. Search terms should appear as natural buyer intent and resource labels, never as backlink or ranking claims.

## Colors

- Canvas: #f6f2ec
- Panel: #ffffff
- Ink: #1f2523
- Muted text: #68716d
- Brand green: #2f5e55
- Deep green: #1f403a
- Warm accent: #9a6a2f
- Soft line: #ded8ce

## Typography

- Primary: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif
- Weight: 700-900 for scene headlines, 400-700 for UI copy

## What NOT to Do

- Do not present the extension as an SEO tool, backlink tool, traffic booster, or ranking automation.
- Do not use keyword stuffing, external-link hype, or claims about search engine ranking.
- Do not imply the extension reads browsing history, page content, tabs, or user data.
- Do not show unsupported website search URL behavior.
- Do not use loud neon, dark hacker styling, or generic SaaS gradient blobs.
